Where it slowly began

Our Story

Some stories do not begin with big moments. Some begin with one glance, one corridor, and one heart quietly looking for the same face again and again.

Chapter 01

The First Glimpse

I still remember the first time I saw you in the department corridor at university. I was from the 21 AI batch, and you were from the 24 AI batch. You were new, graceful, and somehow impossible to ignore.

That first glance stayed with me. After that day, my eyes had learned a new habit. They started searching for you.

Chapter 02

The Welcome Party

Then came the welcome party for the 24 batch. You were shining that day in a way I still remember clearly.

There were many people around, but my eyes were looking for only one person. Slowly, seeing you quietly became a part of my routine.

Chapter 03

The Instagram Excuse

I wanted a reason to talk to you, so I found one. I asked you to send me the welcome party pictures and videos on Instagram.

It looked like a simple request, but for me it was the beginning of a connection I quietly wanted to grow.

Chapter 04

The Little Moments

I used to encourage you to be strong. When you walked with your head down, I would tell you to walk with your head held high.

Sometimes I teased you too, like telling you that juniors should greet their senior. You did it once, maybe out of fear, maybe out of innocence, but that tiny moment still feels sweet to me.

Chapter 05

The Pen Incident

Then came that exam day at the canteen. My friend needed a pen, and I came to you because I thought it would be simple since we already talked.

Later, I understood that the moment became uncomfortable for you because your friends started teasing you. I did not fully understand it then, but I understand it better now, and your feelings matter to me.

Chapter 06

The Silence

After that, there was distance. Conversations faded, and things became quiet between us for some time.

But even in that quiet, some part of me still wanted to talk to you again. So I tried in small ways, through reels, reactions, and tiny chances that might open the door again.

Chapter 07

When Conversation Returned

Then one day, you asked me for help with calculus and for a YouTube channel for exam preparation.

Maybe it was just a study question, but for me it felt like conversation had found a way back. I teased you, helped you, and inside my heart, I became happy again.

Chapter 08

The Farewell

Then came my farewell. Four years of my bachelor’s journey were ending, and you arrived looking so beautiful that I still remember that moment clearly.

You had a charm that day that stayed in my eyes. Then somehow, you appeared in one of my pictures in the background, and that little coincidence became another sweet reason for us to talk.

Chapter 09

From Talking To Loving

After that, we slowly started talking almost every day. Without either of us fully realizing when it happened, feelings started growing.

I thank Allah for placing this love between us. What we have feels deep, sincere, and precious to me.

Chapter 10

The Family Feeling

One of the most beautiful things for me is that my mother, my two younger sisters, and my little brother know about you.

My mother and sisters like you, and seeing that bond makes me genuinely happy. It feels like Allah has shown me a glimpse of the complete family my heart wants.

Chapter 11

My Prayer

My prayer is simple. May Allah always keep us together, keep our hearts soft for each other, and never let distance become stronger than the love He placed between us.

This Story Is Still Ours

I do not want one fight to become bigger than everything we have lived, felt, and prayed for.
