Chaand Raat, But My Moon Is Missing

چاند کے بنا چاند رات کیسی؟

Tonight is Chaand Raat, a night of lights, prayers, smiles, celebration, and warmth. But even with all its beauty, my heart keeps asking one simple thing.

How can my Chaand Raat feel complete when my moon is not smiling with me?

My Chaand Raat Wish

If Chaand Raat is about light, then you are the light my heart searches for. If tonight is about prayers, then my prayer is for your happiness, your peace, your smile, and for the softness between us to return.

May Allah remove the heaviness between us. May He put gentleness back into our words. May He protect the love we have, and may tomorrow bring you an Eid that feels peaceful, beautiful, and full of little reasons to smile.

For My Chaand

The moon may decorate the sky tonight,
but the light I search for still lives in you.

Tomorrow may bring celebration,
but tonight my heart only asks for your smile.

Some people wait for the moon to appear,
I wait for my Alishba to smile again.

My Little Chaand Raat Confession

My darling, my cutie, my ladoo, my kadoo, my danedaar, my tharki junior, even when there is silence between us, you are still the first person my heart wants to reach.

I do not want tomorrow to begin with distance. I want it to begin with a softer heart, a calmer conversation, and maybe one little smile from you that makes everything feel alive again.

A Small Wish For Tomorrow

I hope your Eid is gentle on your heart. I hope your day is filled with peace, sweetness, family, laughter, good food, beautiful moments, and the kind of happiness that stays.

Somewhere in the middle of all that beauty, I hope my name crosses your heart softly, not with anger, not with distance, but with a little warmth, a little smile, and maybe a little thought that we should not let silence win.

May Your Eid Be Beautiful, Alishba

May tomorrow bring you peace, love, comfort, and a heart that feels light again.
