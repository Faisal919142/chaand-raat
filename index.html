Why I Made This

I know silence can become heavy. I know a fight can make two hearts feel far even when the love is still there. I did not make this website to win an argument. I made it because you matter more than the fight.

This is my way of reaching out softly, honestly, and lovingly — because I do not want distance between us. I want us to talk, understand, smile again, and return to the love that has always been bigger than our anger.
